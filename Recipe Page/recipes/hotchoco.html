Hot Chocolate

This hot chocolate with sweetened condensed milk is easy to make. Keep some condensed milk on hand for cold days, and whip up a batch of this rich hot chocolate to warm your loved ones.

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) can Eagle Brand sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 6 ½ cups hot water
  • 3 mini marshmallows

Procedure

  1. Combine sweetened condensed milk, cocoa, vanilla, and salt in a large saucepan over medium heat
  2. mix well
  3. Slowly stir in hot water
  4. cook until heated through, stirring occasionally
  5. Do not boil
  6. Top with marshmallows
  7. Store covered in the refrigerator.

Bonus Tips

You can store hot chocolate in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. Mix well and reheat before serving.

To make hot chocolate in the microwave, combine all ingredients except marshmallows in a 2-quart glass measure. Microwave on high (100% power) for 8 to 10 minutes, stirring every 3 minutes. Top with marshmallows and store covered in the refrigerator.
